Samantha and her loudness

Samantha Gorton, Sam, a VERY loud person who is impossible to get to shut up . If I were told to stop laughing I would stop, sit still for a moment, and burst out into an even louder more obnoxious laugh than before. See I like to live in the moment and if I am told stop living in the moment, I will not like it and continue to laugh in your face.

I like being the loudness, I like everyone hearing everything I have to say, and I like being the leader. I will voice my opinions and share the ways I think things should be done. Call me controlling, I dare you, because I will argue you on it.

Arguing. a key part of my personality. I love arguing people, I mean why wouldn't I? You can be loud with people and agree with them and then, just prove them wrong, and being right is an amazing feeling.
